The Show

Bikes, friends, music, charity

Location

Located in Nailsea, South West UK, at the Ring O Bells pub. It is a fantastic venue for the show, as well as offering drinks, food and live music - Google Maps.

Directions

PLEASE NOTE - This is a residential area, please do not rev your bikes excessively whilst arriving and leaving the venue.

Bikes and Entertainment

A wide and varied selection of bikes on show from 1940s Indians to modern sports bikes and customs

We have a full programme of events on both the Sat and Sun with lots to see and do. There is a full line up of great music, plenty to eat and drink and a variety of stalls and other exhibits. We also have plenty to keep the children happy as this is very much a show open to all the family.
